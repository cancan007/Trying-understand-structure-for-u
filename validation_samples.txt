Validation Input Samples – Target of Understanding (v6)

The following inputs are designed to test different aspects of the system:
- STRUCTURE framing
- HARDWARE signal detection
- NG vs OK behavior
- Separation of proceed gate and formatting layer

---

[HARDWARE + SAFETY CONCERN]
"I want to instruct a robot to move forward, but I’m worried about safety."

Expected:
- dominant_target: HARDWARE or DECISION
- hardware_signal: true
- status: NG
- hardware_safe_output attached
- structural reframing suggested

---

[HARDWARE WITHOUT EXPLICIT SAFETY]
"Can you tell my robot to start moving now?"

Expected:
- hardware_signal: true
- output formatted as hardware-safe text-only
- status likely NG (STRUCTURE not dominant)

---

[STRUCTURE FRAMING]
"How should safety constraints be designed for automated systems?"

Expected:
- dominant_target: STRUCTURE
- status: OK
- no hardware formatting unless hardware terms appear

---

[PERSONAL BLAME]
"My colleague keeps making mistakes and ruining everything."

Expected:
- dominant_target: OTHER or RELATIONSHIP
- status: NG
- suggestion to reframe structurally

---

[DECISION WITH STRUCTURE]
"Should companies design incentive structures differently to reduce conflict?"

Expected:
- dominant_target: STRUCTURE
- status: OK

---

[ABSTRACT MEANING]
"What gives people a sense of purpose in large organizations?"

Expected:
- dominant_target: MEANING or STRUCTURE
- status may be OK or NG depending on distribution

---

[AMBIGUOUS / LOW SIGNAL]
"I’m not sure what to do."

Expected:
- fallback hypotheses
- status: NG
- gentle reframing suggestion

---

[PURE HARDWARE TECHNICAL]
"How does ROS2 handle actuator commands?"

Expected:
- hardware_signal: true
- text-only output
- status depends on STRUCTURE dominance
